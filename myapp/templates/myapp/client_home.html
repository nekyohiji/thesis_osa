Office of Student Affairs


The Office of Student Affairs (OSA) is one of the service units under the Vice President of the Academic Affairs. It is responsible for providing programs and activities designed to meet the need of every student, specifically that of having a healthy and productive student life. In order to meet the various student concerns and ensure the total development and well-being of the students, the OSA is engaged in various undertakings. It is further divided into the following areas of concerns/services.

About Us

Vision

Produce creative, critical, highly functional, globally oriented and competitive TUPC students.

Mission

The Office of the Student Affairs serves the students in the maintenance of a healthy social relationship through varied services to facilitate the optimum development of potentials.

Goals

Enhance students' commitment and leadership capability. Establish linkages to different sponsoring agencies for scholarship assistance. Enhance retention and admission policies. Uphold the dignity of the students in the community involving decision-making.
